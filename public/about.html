About Me

Greetings! My name is Anastasia, and I'm delighted to welcome you to my corner of the web. At 23 years young, I find joy in the everyday adventures that life has to offer. Born on December 24th, 2000, amidst the festive spirit, I bring a sprinkle of holiday cheer to every occasion.

Hailing from the picturesque island of Cyprus, I embrace the rich blend of cultures that define my identity. With a British mother and a Cypriot father, I've inherited a love for both tea and halloumi, a delightful mix indeed.

A graduate of Cyprus College, I hold a diploma in aesthetics and cosmetology, where I've honed my skills in the art of enhancing natural beauty. Whether it's perfecting a flawless look or mastering the latest skincare techniques.

But beyond the realm of beauty, I thrive on the simple pleasures of life. I cherish moments spent with cherished friends, whether it's savoring a cup of coffee, catching a flick at the cinema, or embarking on adrenaline-pumping adventures. From water sports to late-night parties, from spontaneous travels to daring escapades, I'm always up for the next thrill.
