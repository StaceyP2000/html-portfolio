The best movies according to Stacey

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Gone girl

Gone Girl is a riveting psychological thriller. When Nick Dunne's (Affleck) wife, Amy (Pike), mysteriously disappears on their fifth wedding anniversary, he becomes the prime suspect. As media attention intensifies, shocking secrets about their marriage emerge, leading to unexpected twists. Known for its suspenseful plot, dark themes, and standout performances, especially Pike's chilling portrayal of Amy, "Gone Girl" is a must-watch for fans of intricate mysteries and psychological drama.

Pretty woman

Pretty Woman is a classic romantic comedy about the unlikely romance between a wealthy businessman and a spirited Hollywood prostitute. Hired to accompany him to social events, their professional arrangement soon becomes something deeper. It's a feel-good movie with a perfect blend of humor, romance, and heartwarming moments. If you want an uplifting love story, "Pretty Woman" is a must-watch.

Shutter Island

Shutter Island is a gripping psychological thriller. It follows U.S. Marshal Teddy Daniels as he investigates a patient's disappearance from a remote mental institution, uncovering shocking secrets and questioning his own sanity. Known for its intense atmosphere, twisty plot, and powerful performances, it's a must-watch if you enjoy dark, suspenseful mysteries.
